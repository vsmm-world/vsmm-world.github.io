<About Me/>

I'm Ravindra Valand — a freelance full-stack developer working out of Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. Over the past 3 years I've built and shipped production systems end-to-end: REST/GraphQL-style APIs, admin dashboards, customer-facing web apps, an Android app, and an Electron desktop client — often as the whole multi-repo product for a single client.

My stack leans NestJS/Express + MongoDB/Prisma on the backend and React on the frontend, with detours into Java/Spring Boot, PHP and Kotlin/Android depending on what the project needs. I lean on AI-assisted tooling (GitHub Copilot, Cursor) to move faster without cutting corners on structure.
